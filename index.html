About us

Why choose us?

We have been in Indonesia and lots of another countries for more than 15 years, teaching more than 6,000 people to scuba dive.

We have small class sizes of max 4 students, often with only 1 or 2 students.

We have full-time, experienced instructors who follow all the PADI guidelines for providing a safe course to the highest quality possible.

We also have contacts with experienced Indonesian instructors who teach according to our principles, and we call upon them for teaching when needed.

Safety is our first concern, and our dive equipment is fully maintained.

We include dive insurance and a dive computer as standard in your course, unlike other centres.

We are a PADI 5 Star center which means that we are committed to offering quality dive courses.
